Privacy Policy for Credential Vault

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

Credential Vault respects your privacy and is designed to keep your credentials secure while minimizing data collection. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the application.

1. Information We Collect

Credential Vault does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to external servers.

All credentials and related data entered into the app are stored locally on the user’s device.

Examples of data stored locally may include:

Account names

Usernames

Passwords

Notes associated with stored credentials

This information never leaves the device unless the user explicitly exports it.

2. Data Storage

All data within Credential Vault is stored locally on the device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system.

The application does not use remote databases, cloud storage, or external servers to store user data.

3. Backup and Export

Credential Vault allows users to create encrypted backups of their stored credentials.

These backups are created only when the user manually chooses to export their data.

The exported backup file remains fully under the user's control and may be stored wherever the user chooses.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

Credential Vault may offer optional subscription features.

Payments and subscription management are handled directly by the platform provider, such as:

Google Play Billing (Android)

Apple In-App Purchases (iOS)

Credential Vault does not collect or store payment information such as credit card numbers or billing details.

Payment information is processed securely by the respective app store according to their privacy policies:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

5. Data Sharing

Credential Vault does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

Because the app does not collect or transmit personal data, no information is shared with:

Advertisers

Analytics providers

Cloud services

Third-party partners

6. Internet Usage

Credential Vault does not require an internet connection to function, except when interacting with app store services for subscriptions or purchases.

The core functionality of the app operates entirely offline.

7. Data Security

Credential Vault is designed with security in mind. Sensitive information stored within the app is protected through encryption and secure storage methods where applicable.

Users are responsible for:

Protecting access to their device

Safeguarding exported backup files

Maintaining device security such as passcodes or biometric locks

8. Children's Privacy

Credential Vault does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Because the app does not collect any personal data, there is no risk of children's data being transmitted or stored externally.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements or regulatory requirements.

Any updates will be reflected by revising the Effective Date at the top of this document.

10. Your Consent

By using Credential Vault, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
