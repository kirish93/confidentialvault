  <h1>Privacy Policy for Credential Vault</h1>
  <p class="meta"><strong>Effective Date:</strong> March 6, 2026</p>

  <p>
    Credential Vault respects your privacy and is designed to keep your credentials secure
    while minimizing data collection. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled
    when you use the application.
  </p>

  <h2>1. Information We Collect</h2>
  <p>
    Credential Vault does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to external servers.
  </p>
  <p>
    All credentials and related data entered into the app are stored locally on the user’s device.
  </p>
  <p>Examples of data stored locally may include:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Account names</li>
    <li>Usernames</li>
    <li>Passwords</li>
    <li>Notes associated with stored credentials</li>
  </ul>
  <p>
    This information never leaves the device unless the user explicitly exports it.
  </p>

  <h2>2. Data Storage</h2>
  <p>
    All data within Credential Vault is stored locally on the device using secure storage
    mechanisms provided by the operating system.
  </p>
  <p>
    The application does not use remote databases, cloud storage, or external servers to store user data.
  </p>

  <h2>3. Backup and Export</h2>
  <p>
    Credential Vault allows users to create encrypted backups of their stored credentials.
  </p>
  <p>
    These backups are created only when the user manually chooses to export their data.
  </p>
  <p>
    The exported backup file remains fully under the user's control and may be stored wherever the user chooses.
  </p>

  <h2>4. Subscriptions and Payments</h2>
  <p>
    Credential Vault may offer optional subscription features.
  </p>
  <p>
    Payments and subscription management are handled directly by the platform provider, such as:
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li>Google Play Billing (Android)</li>
    <li>Apple In-App Purchases (iOS)</li>
  </ul>
  <p>
    Credential Vault does not collect or store payment information such as credit card numbers or billing details.
  </p>
  <p>
    Payment information is processed securely by the respective app store according to their privacy policies:
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li><a href="https://policies.google.com/privacy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Google Privacy Policy</a></li>
    <li><a href="https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Apple Privacy Policy</a></li>
  </ul>

  <h2>5. Data Sharing</h2>
  <p>
    Credential Vault does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.
  </p>
  <p>
    Because the app does not collect or transmit personal data, no information is shared with:
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li>Advertisers</li>
    <li>Analytics providers</li>
    <li>Cloud services</li>
    <li>Third-party partners</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>6. Internet Usage</h2>
  <p>
    Credential Vault does not require an internet connection to function, except when interacting
    with app store services for subscriptions or purchases.
  </p>
  <p>
    The core functionality of the app operates entirely offline.
  </p>

  <h2>7. Data Security</h2>
  <p>
    Credential Vault is designed with security in mind. Sensitive information stored within the app
    is protected through encryption and secure storage methods where applicable.
  </p>
  <p>Users are responsible for:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Protecting access to their device</li>
    <li>Safeguarding exported backup files</li>
    <li>Maintaining device security such as passcodes or biometric locks</li>
  </ul>
